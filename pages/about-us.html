About Us

Information Security Analytics LLC was founded in 2012 to provide research and development into innovative approaches to to information security defense. Infosec Analytics focuses on developing this infosec intellectual property and transfering it to the information security community to help defend against attacks.

Information Security Analytics pursues multiple approaches to intellectual property transfer. These include sharing through speaking engagements, blogs, and publication. It also includes patent protection of some IP for development and licensing to interested parties. Finally, Infosec Analytics provides consulting services to facilitate the improvement of information security defense.

Information Security Analytics is a small business borne on years of technical knowledge and information security experience. Infosec Analytics loves solving hard problems. We attempt to provide solutions harmonizing engineering, intelligence, and operations through improvements in architecture, implementation, testing, and operations.
